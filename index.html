OUR PROCESS
1. Seller delivers gold to the refinery

The seller delivers the gold to Emir Gold Refinery Limited, where it is weighed at our secure intake counter. Thorough tests are conducted to assess its quality and purity. This begins our rigorous authentication process, ensuring the gold meets required standards before proceeding to refining and further verification.

2. The gold is then melted

The gold is melted using a high-precision induction furnace. This smelting process transforms the metal into a liquid state, preparing it for final purity testing and verification.

3. The melted gold is cast into a fresh mold, returned to the counter

The liquefied gold is carefully poured into a clean mold, allowed to cool, and then returned to the counter. Once solidified, it undergoes a second weighing, followed by a detailed assay and additional purity testing to confirm the results of the initial evaluation.

4. Seller receives a receipt

At the final stage, the seller is issued a detailed receipt at the cash counter, outlining the gold's weight, purity grade, and corresponding payout amount. This transparent documentation ensures that sellers have a clear and verifiable record of their transaction, confirming the quantity, quality, and value of the gold sold.

5. Cashier pays the Seller in full

Upon presentation of the receipt, which includes the gold’s weight, purity grade, and payout details, the cashier promptly issues full payment to the seller. This ensures a smooth and transparent transaction, with sellers receiving immediate compensation based on the verified and documented terms of the sale.
