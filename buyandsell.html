Buy and Sell Gold

Emir Gold Refinery Limited welcomes you to carry out your gold trading activities within our state-of-the-art, highly secured facility. We are fully licensed and operate under a valid Mineral Trading Permit for precious metals, in accordance with the Republic of Zambia’s 2015 Mines and Minerals Development Act. At Emir, we prioritize integrity in every transaction, ensuring the protection of buyers’ funds and the authenticity of every gold purchase. Your trust and security are our highest priorities

Buyers

Emir Gold Refinery Limited offers a highly secure environment for buyers to purchase gold in Lusaka, Zambia. We fully understand the challenges and risks involved in acquiring precious metals within African jurisdictions. With this in mind, we have designed a robust, multi-layered transaction process that prioritizes the security of your investment and the authenticity of your gold.

Our process is both foolproof and transparent, incorporating several levels of verification to ensure maximum protection. Buyer funds are never released to the seller until every stage of our rigorous Quality Assurance and Quality Control (QA/QC) protocol is thoroughly completed, independently verified, and personally approved by both the President of Emir and our Chief Metallurgist.

Sellers

Emir Gold Refinery Limited ensures a swift and seamless transaction experience, offering immediate settlement services designed to earn your continued trust and business. We are committed to full transparency, guaranteeing that you are accurately compensated for every gram of gold brought to our facility for sale. As a seller, you will have uninterrupted visual access to your gold throughout the verification process, with real-time visibility of all data and testing procedures.

Please note: All materials submitted for refining must be accompanied by a clear legal title, as sellers are required to warrant lawful ownership of the gold presented.
